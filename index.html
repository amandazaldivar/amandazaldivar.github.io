Creative Computing

Welcome to my online collection of the projects in my Creative Computing class! Feel free to browse.

  • Interview Website

    Here, the class held one on one interviews, and compiled all of our data to make individual websites. With basic HTML and CSS, I constructed a simple website with everybody's interview.

  • Public Service Announcement

    In this assignment, we were tasked to create a webpage that informs the public about an issue. With no imagery allowed, and expanding on CSS skills, I made a PSA focusing on annoying tourists getting in the way in the New York City streets.

  • JavaScript Composition--Iteration 1

    Here, we began to learn about the possibilities of JavaScript. We were told to make a beautiful composition.

  • JavaScript Composition-- Iteration 2

    Expanding off of my first composition, I added a new element: random dots that change position with every refresh.

  • JavaScript Composition-- Iteration 3

    My third and final iteration: the center circle grows by the second, and then restarts at the minute mark.
